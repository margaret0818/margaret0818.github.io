WeWalk


WeWalk

This was me and and a few friends’ project for HackUTD 2018. As female freshman students at the University of Texas at Austin, we were fully aware of the unfortunate reality of the dangerousness of walking alone across an empty campus and its surrounding areas late at night. Therefore, we wanted to build something to help people walk home with a better sense of security.


What is it?

This app pairs two users walking in the same direction as walking buddies. The University Police Department would obtain the location of a user for max safety. Each user is provided info about other potential walking buddies (such as their classification and major) so there is transparency between each party using the app.


How it was built

We chose to build an iOS app due to the prevalence of college students with iOS devices as their main source of communication. We also needed a database to store user info for the matching component of the application. For this, we used Firebase to collect each user’s info as they register/log-in. We used Xcode to build the application.
